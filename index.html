Welcome to Cashflow Compass

This app forecasts your money up to 36 months ahead. Tell it your accounts, paycheck, and recurring expenses — it shows the month-by-month picture, where every dollar goes, and how long until things get tight.

  1. Add an account with its current balance
  2. Add your recurring transactions — paycheck (bi-weekly works), rent, subscriptions, etc.
  3. Pick how far to look ahead at the top right (End of Year through 36 months)

All data stays in your browser. Export to Excel any time as a portable backup.
